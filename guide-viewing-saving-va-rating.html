The Process

The 3-Click Path to Your Rating

1

Log In to VA.gov

Use your Login.gov or ID.me account. (See our previous guide if you don't have one!).

2

Click "Disability"

This is usually in the main menu bar or a large icon on the dashboard called "Check your claim, decision review, or appeal status".

3

Select "View your VA disability ratings"

Don't click "Claims." Look specifically for the word Rating. This page will show your big "Combined Rating" percentage at the top.

How to Capture the Proof

Once you see your rating on the screen, how do you save it? Choose your device below.

üíª

Windows PC

The easiest way is the "Snipping Tool".

  1. Press Windows Logo Key + Shift + S.
  2. Your screen will go dark.
  3. Click and drag a box around your rating number.
  4. It is now copied! Paste it into an email or Word doc.
üçé

Apple Mac

Use the keyboard shortcut.

  1. Press Command + Shift + 4.
  2. Your cursor turns into a crosshair.
  3. Click and drag a box around your rating.
  4. The image will save to your Desktop automatically.

iPhone / Android

Two-button combination.

  • iPhone: Press Power (right side) and Volume Up (left side) at the same time.
  • Android: Press Power and Volume Down at the same time.

Proof of Rating: Screenshot vs. Letter

Official Letter Screenshot

"Can I Use a Screenshot for a Mortgage?"

Probably NOT.

Screenshots are great for quick discounts (like Lowe's or Home Depot) or State Park passes. However, for serious financial business (VA Loans, Property Tax Exemptions), you need the Official Benefit Summary Letter.

How to get the Official Letter:

On the same VA.gov dashboard, instead of clicking "Disability", look for the box that says "Letters" or "Download your benefit letters". This generates a PDF signed by the VA Commissioner.
