VA Disability Claim FAQs

Answers to common questions about private medical evidence coordination, claim preparation, and support boundaries.

What does TYFYS do?

We coordinate private medical evidence (DBQs and independent medical opinions) to support veterans disability claims.

Does TYFYS file VA claims or provide legal representation?

No. We do not file VA claims, provide legal advice, or provide legal representation.

How long does the process usually take?

Timelines vary by records readiness, provider scheduling, and claim complexity. VA decision timelines are always controlled by the VA.

What documents should I gather first?

Start with DD-214, service treatment records, current diagnosis records, medication lists, and prior VA decision letters.

Do you guarantee an approval or rating increase?

No. We do not guarantee outcomes. All claim decisions are made by the VA.

Is pricing percentage-based?

No. We use a flat-fee model and do not take percentages of VA backpay.

Documentation Checklist for Evidence Packages

  • DD-214 (Member-4 copy if available)
  • Service treatment records and deployment notes
  • Current diagnosis and treatment records
  • Prior VA rating decisions and denial letters
