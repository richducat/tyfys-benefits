The "Fully Developed Claim" Strategy

We don't just "submit" forms and hope for the best. We build a bulletproof case that often allows you to skip the dreaded C&P exams.

1

Discovery & Audit

Zero Cost

We dig deep. We don't just ask what hurts; we "squeeze and drain" your military records to find every overlooked condition. We check your eligibility, review your current rating, and identify the specific medical gaps causing your denials.

Goal: To identify "High Value" claims you didn't even know you had.
2

Strategy & Game Plan

Transparent

We present a clear roadmap. You'll know exactly which conditions we are targeting (e.g., Tinnitus, PTSD, Back Pain) and the potential rating increase. We explain our Flat-Fee Investment structure clearlyâ€”so you know the ROI before we start.

  • No hidden percentages.
  • Flexible payment options discussed upfront.
3

Private Medical Evidence

The "Secret Weapon"

This is the core of our service. We connect you with private doctors via Telehealth who understand VA codes. They write the DBQs and Nexus Letters that explicitly link your injury to your service.

Why this matters:

This evidence is often enough to bypass the risky C&P exam, putting the control back in your hands.

4

Submission & Decision

30-60 Days Avg

We submit a "Fully Developed Claim." Because we provided all the evidence upfront, the VA can process your claim significantly faster. We track it daily until you get your rating decision.

Outcome Protection: If we don't win, we keep fighting for you at no extra charge.

A Simple, Transparent Investment

We believe you should keep your backpay. That's why we use a flat-fee structure instead of taking a percentage of your check forever.
