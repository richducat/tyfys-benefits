Back, Neck & Joint Claims

Proven strategies to link your current pain to your past service, even if you've been out for years.

The "Secondary Condition" Strategy

Most veterans have a low rating for a single issue (e.g., 10% for a knee). But they ignore the chain reaction. If your knee makes you walk with a limp, and that limp hurts your back, your back pain is service-connected.

How We Win: We use specialized Nexus Letters to prove "Secondary Service Connection." We show the medical link between your rated condition and your unrated pain, unlocking higher ratings.
