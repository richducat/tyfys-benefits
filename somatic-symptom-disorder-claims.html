19. Somatic Symptom Disorder: When Pain Becomes Mental

Keywords: Chronic Pain Syndrome, SSD, Mental Health Claims

If you have chronic pain (back, knees, shoulders) that has lasted for years, it likely affects your mental health. This is a claimable condition often called Somatic Symptom Disorder (SSD).

It links your physical pain to your mental health rating. It is a powerful way to bridge the gap for veterans who have physical injuries but were never diagnosed with "PTSD" during service.
