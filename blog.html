Phase 2: Decision

Choose Your Path

You have two main doors to enter the VA.gov house. Both work perfectly fine, but they have different strengths.

Option A: Login.gov

Run by the government. Simple, direct, and usually the best place to start for most veterans.

Option B: ID.me

A private partner. Offers more robust support options, including video calls and UPS store visits.

TYFYS Recommendation: Try Login.gov first. If you get stuck, switch to ID.me for their offline support.

Digital Account Creation

A

Creating a Login.gov Account

The primary government pathway.

Click "Sign In" at the top right, then select "Create an account with Login.gov".

Phase 4: Alternatives

Struggling with the Camera?

You are not alone. Many veterans prefer to verify their identity in person rather than fighting with a smartphone camera.

Option A: The UPS Store (For ID.me)

  • Select "Verify in Person" on the ID.me screen.
  • Write down your Registration Code.
  • Bring your Code, ID, and a Utility Bill to the store.

Option B: The Post Office (For Login.gov)

  • Select "Verify at a Post Office" on Login.gov.
  • You will get a Barcode via email.
  • Bring the Barcode and your State ID to a participating USPS location.

Verification Method Popularity

In-person verification is a standard and respected choice.
