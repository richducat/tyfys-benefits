TYFYS App Support

Last updated: March 31, 2026

TYFYS helps veterans organize records, complete onboarding, upload claim-related documents, and manage next steps for private medical evidence development that may support a VA disability claim. TYFYS does not file VA claims, provide legal representation, or offer legal advice.

TYFYS is operated by TYFYS MANAGEMENT LLC.

Common Support Topics

  • Account sign-in, password reset, and duplicate-account issues.
  • Document uploads, saved progress, and onboarding questions.
  • Questions about TYFYS services, scheduling, or next steps in your workspace.
  • Requests related to privacy, account access, or account deletion.

Need Help Accessing Your Account?

Start from the TYFYS sign-in screen if you need to reset your password. If you cannot access the email address on file or you believe your account was created under the wrong email, contact TYFYS support and include your full name, the best callback number, and the email address tied to your account if known.

Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your TYFYS account or submitted information, visit the account and data deletion page. If you only want to remove information stored locally on your device, sign out and delete the TYFYS app from that device.
