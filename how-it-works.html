How It Works: Private Medical Evidence Timeline

This timeline explains what to expect from intake through evidence readiness. Timing varies by case complexity, record availability, and provider scheduling.

  1. 1. Discovery Call and Eligibility Review

    We review your objectives, existing ratings, and likely evidence gaps.

  2. 2. Records Collection and Evidence Mapping

    We map required records and define which conditions need DBQ and opinion support.

  3. 3. Provider Coordination

    Coordinate private provider reviews and documentation requests for supported conditions.

  4. 4. Draft Package QA

    Quality-check packet structure, record completeness, and clinical rationale consistency.

  5. 5. Final Evidence Package Handoff

    You receive a submission-ready package and checklist for your chosen filing path.

  6. 6. Post-Handoff Questions

    We clarify package contents and next-step logistics. VA claim decisions and timelines remain VA-controlled.

What to Prepare Before Intake

  • DD-214 and service treatment records
  • Recent diagnosis and treatment records
  • Prior VA decisions and denial letters
  • Condition list prioritized by symptom impact
