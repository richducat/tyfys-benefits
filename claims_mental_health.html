Mental Health & PTSD Claims

The most common high-value claim. Stop settling for 30% when you deserve 70% or 100%.

Why Your Claim Was Denied (Or Underrated)

Mental health claims like PTSD, Depression, and Anxiety are subjective. The VA C&P examiners often spend less than 15 minutes with you. They check boxes without understanding the daily impact on your life, work, and relationships.

The Solution: We connect you with a private Psychologist or Psychiatrist via Telehealth. They spend the time to fully evaluate you and complete a detailed DBQ (Disability Benefits Questionnaire) that reflects the true severity of your condition.
