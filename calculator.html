Calculator FAQ

Does the calculator replace official VA decisions?

No. The calculator is a planning tool. Only the VA assigns final disability ratings and effective dates.

Can this show why I am stuck below 100%?

Yes. It helps show how close you are to the next rating threshold so you can focus on the evidence gaps with the biggest impact.

What should I do after I use the calculator?

Review your results, compare them to your current medical documentation, and then decide whether you need records, a DBQ, a nexus letter, or a new claim strategy.

TYFYS coordinates private medical evidence, including DBQs and independent medical opinions, to support veterans disability claims. TYFYS does not provide legal advice, representation, or file VA claims.
