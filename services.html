VA Disability Evidence Services

TYFYS coordinates private medical evidence development to support VA disability claims, including DBQs and independent medical opinions. We do not file claims, provide legal representation, or offer legal advice.

Records & Gap Audit

Service record and current evidence review to identify missing medical-link documentation.

DBQ Coordination

Coordinate provider documentation in DBQ format for supported conditions.

Independent Medical Opinions

Coordinate nexus-style medical rationale where clinical linkage is needed.

Transparent Pricing: What Is Included

Pricing is quote-based and depends on condition count and evidence complexity. Typical scopes include:

  • Evidence scope planning and condition prioritization
  • Medical-document packet review before final delivery
  • Provider coordination for DBQ and opinion requests
  • File readiness checklist for your own claim submission
